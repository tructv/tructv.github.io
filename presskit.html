Description : 3dSenVR is a unique NES emulator that converts classic and homebrew 2D NES games into full 3D experiences in realtime and let you play them in VR. It is developed by Tran Vu Truc since 2015. For the Steam Eearly Access release, 48 of the most popular classic and homebrew games are supported. Light gun games like Duck Hunt, Wild GunMan, Hogan's Alley are also supported.

Highlight Features

Immersive Vision : realtime lighting & shadow over voxel models combined with customized animations produces a completely new and immersive visual experience compared to the original 2D one.

6 Degree of Freedom : capacity of free moving makes you feel like being in the game and becoming a part of it

Zero Input Lag : pressing any button and the game will respond immediately just like how you play the game in the original NES

Lightgun Support : using motion controller as the NES zapper for lightgun games
