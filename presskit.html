Description

3dSenVR (formerly known as 3dNesVR) is an emulator for NES game console which is capable to convert 2D games into 3D in realtime and allow playing it in VR. It is developed solely by Tran Vu Truc since 2015. For the Steam EA release, 48 classic Super Mario Bros; Contra; Legend Of Zelda; MegaMan 1,2; Metroid; Shadow of Ninja, Castlevania ...) and homebrew games (Battle Kid, Super Bat Puncher, Micro Mages ...) are supported. Light gun games like Duck Hunt, Wild GunMan, Hogan's Alley are also supported. More games will be supported in later updates.

Highlight Features

Immersive Vision

Realtime lighting & shadow over voxel models and customized animations will produce a completely new and refreshing visual experience compared to the original 2D one.

6 Degree of Freedom

You are basically in the game yourself

Zero Input Lag

Press a button and the game will respond immediately just like you are playing the original console

Lightgun Support

Use motion controller as the NES zapper for lightgun games
