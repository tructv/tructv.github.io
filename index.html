009 / FAQ

Things people
actually ask.

Do I need original NES games? +

Yes. 3dSen ships without any ROMs, in line with copyright. Bring your own legally-acquired .nes files and 3dSen handles the rest.

Does the 3D break the original gameplay? +

No. Every byte of the original NES game runs untouched. The 3D layer is a presentation pass — physics, timing, and behaviour are 100% authentic.

PC vs VR — which one should I buy? +

If you don't own a headset, get 3dSen PC. If you do, 3dSen VR includes both PC and VR modes in one purchase.

Which VR headsets are supported? +

Anything that runs SteamVR. Tested extensively on Meta Quest 2/3, Pico 4 Ultra, Pimax Crystal Light, Valve Index, and HTC Vive.

My favourite game isn't on the list — what now? +

Use 3dSenMaker to build a 3D profile yourself, or request the title on the Steam community boards. New profiles ship regularly.

Will my old laptop run it? +

Probably. The PC version targets a Core 2 Duo or equivalent, 4 GB RAM, ~400 MB disk. Windows, macOS and Linux supported.
