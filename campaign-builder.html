Naming conventions

Parameter Use for Examples
utm_source The platform sending the click tiktok, youtube, reddit, twitter, facebook, instagram, discord, newsletter, streamer
utm_medium The type of placement on that platform paid_social, organic_social, paid_search, display, email, referral, influencer, press
utm_campaign A specific marketing campaign or push launch_q2_2026, vr_motion_q2_2026, summer_sale_2026, evergreen_demo
utm_content The specific creative or variant mario_pipes_15s, zelda_diorama_9s, punchout_tyson_30s
utm_term The targeted audience or keyword retro_gaming, nes_nostalgia, vr_users, indie_dev

Rules: lowercase only, use underscores instead of spaces or hyphens, no special characters. Stick to the convention — Steam treats TikTok, tiktok, and tik_tok as three different sources.
